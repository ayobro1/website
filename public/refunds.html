Sales & Refunds

Last updated: November 21, 2024

1. Payment Structure

Our standard payment structure is as follows:

  • 50% Deposit: Required before work begins
  • 50% Final Payment: Due upon project completion and before final delivery
  • Custom payment plans available for projects over $10,000

2. Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Bank Transfer (Wire Transfer)
  • Cryptocurrency (for projects over $5,000)

3. Refund Policy

We stand behind the quality of our work. Our refund policy is as follows:

Deposit Refunds

  • Before work begins: 100% refund minus processing fees
  • Within first week: 75% refund if no substantial work completed
  • After work begins: Deposits are non-refundable

Final Payment Refunds

  • Before delivery: Full refund if project not delivered
  • After delivery: Refunds considered on case-by-case basis
  • Quality issues: We'll fix issues at no charge or provide partial refund

4. Cancellation Policy

Either party may cancel the project with written notice:

Client Cancellation

  • Client pays for all work completed to date
  • Hourly rate applied based on package pricing
  • Deposit is non-refundable after work begins
  • Client receives all work completed up to cancellation date

PixelPerfect Cancellation

  • Full refund of all payments made
  • Client retains all work completed
  • Rare circumstances only (scope creep, unprofessional behavior, etc.)

5. Revision Policy

Each package includes a set number of revision rounds:

  • Identity Package: 2 revision rounds
  • Business Package: 3 revision rounds
  • Commerce Package: 4 revision rounds
  • Additional revisions: $150/hour

6. Scope Changes

Changes to the original project scope will be quoted separately. Significant scope changes may require:

  • Additional payment before proceeding
  • Revised timeline and delivery date
  • New contract or amendment to existing contract

7. Late Payment

Late payments are subject to the following:

  • 5% late fee after 7 days
  • Additional 5% fee every 7 days thereafter
  • Work may be paused until payment is received
  • Final deliverables withheld until full payment

8. Satisfaction Guarantee

We're committed to your satisfaction. If you're not happy with the final product:

  • We'll work with you to address concerns
  • Additional revisions at no charge (within reason)
  • Partial refund if we can't meet your expectations
  • Full refund in cases of gross negligence or failure to deliver

9. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact us via email or contact form
  2. Provide detailed reason for refund request
  3. We'll review within 3-5 business days
  4. Approved refunds processed within 7-10 business days
  5. Refunds issued to original payment method

10. Disputes

In case of disputes regarding payment or refunds:

  • Contact us first to resolve amicably
  • Mediation available through neutral third party
  • Arbitration as final resort
  • Legal action governed by our Terms of Use
